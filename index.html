How Payment Works

Your money moves only when you say so.

When you place an order, your payment is held in a secure escrow account — not released to the designer until you are satisfied. Once the courier arrives and you have tried the piece on, you decide. Confirm the fit and funds transfer instantly. Not happy? Hand it back on the spot for a complete refund. No waiting. No disputes.

Payment held in escrow
Funds are secured at checkout. The designer is notified to prepare your order.
Courier delivers to you

You approve the fit
Love it? Confirm with one tap. Not for you? Hand it back to the courier for an immediate full refund.
Payment released to designer
Funds transfer to the designer instantly upon your confirmation. Transparent and traceable at every stage.
